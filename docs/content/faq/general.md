# General

## Can I invite Bot to my server?

A publicly hosted Bot is planned. For now you can [self-host Bot](../selfhost/index.md) on your own infrastructure.

## Is Bot free?

Yes. Bot is free and open source under the [GPL-3.0](https://github.com/awfixers-stuff/bot/blob/main/LICENSE) license. You can use, modify, and contribute to it without cost.

## How can I support Bot?

- **Contribute Code:** Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/awfixers-stuff/bot/issues) or send a pull request.
- **Discuss and Brainstorm:** Jump into our [Discord](https://discord.gg/gpmSjcjQxg) server and reach out.
- **Donate Financially:** Donate to the AWFixer Enterprising Inc [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/awfixers-stuff).

## Can you add a specific feature?

Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/awfixers-stuff/bot/issues) with the `[FEATURE]` prefix, or send a pull request. We welcome ideas from the community.

## Where can I see the roadmap?

The [roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/awfixers-stuff/projects/13) is tracked in GitHub. It outlines planned features and the path to v0.1.0.
